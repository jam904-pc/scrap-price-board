Live Scrap Price Board

This board fetches the latest available scrap price indices from the Federal Reserve (FRED) and scales them to local prices. Materials are sorted by current price per pound, with green rows indicating a price increase and red rows indicating a decrease compared to the previous observation. Supply your own FRED API key for automatic updates; without a key the board falls back to static data.
